Akan Names

Akan Names are derived from Ghananian culture. Frequently, in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' that corresponds to the day in the week they were born. The naming goes as follows:


Male names
  • Sunday - Kwasi
  • Monday - Kwadwo
  • Tuesday - Kwabena
  • Wednesday - Kwaku
  • Friday - Yaw
  • Saturday - Kofi
  • Sunday -Kwame
Female Names
  • Sunday - Akosua
  • Monday - Adwoa
  • Tuesday - Abenaa
  • Wednesday - Akua
  • Friday - Yaa
  • Saturday - Afua
  • Sunday - Ama
